
Chapter 9 :: Optimizing Our App


===> Single Responsibility Principle
Ans: this means that a component should encapsulate and handle a single, well-defined concern or responsibility within your software system.
     It should not have multiple unrelated responsibilities or concerns bundled together.
	  
===> Advantages Single Responsibility Principle
Ans: * Improved Maintainability : When a code has a single responsibility, it becomes easier to maintain it.
     * Enhanced Reusability : 
	 * Better Testability
	 * Reduced Risk of Bugs
